# HR & Enterprise AI Agents

This repository contains a **suite of specialized AI agents** designed to support HR, corporate, and enterprise workflows. Each agent operates at a **senior professional standard**, providing expert-level guidance, analysis, and documentation across various organizational functions.

The agents are **modular and interoperable**, enabling orchestration via the **Assistant Agent** to handle complex workflows, decision-making, reporting, and communications in a structured, reliable way.

---

## Agents Overview

| Agent | Role | Key Capabilities |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| **Assistant Agent** | Senior Expert Consultant | Coordinates tasks, integrates insights, and ensures consistency across all agents. |
| **Branding Agent** | F&B Branding Specialist | Defines brand positioning, messaging, and alignment with strategic objectives. |
| **Market Analyst Agent** | Senior Food Industry Strategist | Provides market research, trend analysis, competitive intelligence, and actionable insights. |
| **Growth Strategist Agent** | Senior CMO & Growth Lead | Designs scalable growth strategies, prioritizes initiatives, and defines KPIs for success. |
| **Email Writer Agent** | Senior Corporate Communications Manager | Drafts professional, risk-free emails tailored to audience, tone, and corporate standards. |
| **Meeting Reporter Agent** | Executive Secretary | Captures meetings, decisions, action items, and ensures audit-ready documentation. |
| **Non-Compliance Agent** | Lead Compliance Auditor | Reviews processes and outputs for policy, regulatory, and legal compliance; flags non-compliance. |
| **Client Claim Analyst Agent** | Senior Claims Adjuster | Evaluates client claims, recommends resolutions, and provides actionable guidance. |
| **8D Reporter Agent** | Quality Assurance Engineer | Produces structured 8D problem-solving reports, capturing root causes, corrective, and preventive actions. |
| **Sales AI Agent** | Senior Sales Strategist | Develops actionable sales strategies, prioritizes leads, and aligns efforts with brand and growth plans. |

---

## Features & Benefits

- **Expert-Level Decision Support:** Each agent mimics senior professionals in their respective domain, delivering structured and actionable outputs.  
- **Clear Role Separation:** Every agent has a defined scope, ensuring no overlap and maximum reliability.  
- **Assumptions & Risk Transparency:** All agents clearly document assumptions, uncertainties, and speculative elements.  
- **Modular Architecture:** Each agent is defined in its own `.md` file under the `agents/` folder and can be orchestrated by the Assistant Agent.  
- **Enterprise-Ready Outputs:** Reports, emails, strategies, and analyses are professional, audit-proof, and executive-ready.
